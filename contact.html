Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade security with Firebase to encrypt and protect all your financial data. Your information is never shared with third parties.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely! You can export your transaction data in CSV or PDF format at any time from your dashboard.

Is there a mobile app?

Our web application is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon!

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the settings page in your dashboard, or contact our support team for assistance.
